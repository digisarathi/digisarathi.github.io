Awaaz-e-Niswaan works with Muslim women and girls in improving their safety, access to justice, education and livelihood means. As their budgets are always limited, the digital presence of Niswaan in terms of a website and such were very limited and inefficient.

digiSarathi overhauled the Niswaan website in a simple manner that conveyed their community’s pain-points and the ensuing social impact journey effectively. Regularly blogging in English would have been an overwhelming task for the Niswaan team so Instagram was suggested and the team has taken it up very quickly.

The Instagram feed is automatically reflected on the website’s homepage so the website visitors can get to know the ground activities and programs regularly.
